The evolution of three-point shots in the NBA

In basketball games, a team earned three-points if the a successful shot is made behind the three-point arc, which is typically 23 feed from the hoop. If a successful shot is made within the three-point arc, the team earned two-points instead. Modern NBA teams rely more and more three-point shots in their offense.

In this visualization, you can explore this trend by clicking your favourite team and the statistics that you would like to explore.

3PAPG: averaged three-pointer attempted per game
